Utilizing the formerly forgotten arts of King Schmitz's elite combat unit, Royal Guards destroy enemies with powerfully enhanced spear and shield skills, while still being a stalwart defender for their allies. They can break specific enemy equipment with Pinpoint Attack, smash foes into walls with Overbrand, and Trample over traps and field effects. Defensively, they can evade magic attacks with Prestige or even grant temporary invulnerability using King's Grace. A group of Royal Guards can also band together to unlock more skills that help support friends and defeat adversaries. [ Class Guides ]
